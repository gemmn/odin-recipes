Frech Toast

Simple Yet Amazing French Toast

Recommended to be served hot with butter or margarine and maple syrup.

Ingredients

  • 6 thick slices bread
  • 2 eggs
  • ⅔ cup milk
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon (Optional)
  • ¼ teaspoon ground nutmeg (Optional)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (Optional)
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. Beat together egg, milk, salt, desired spices and vanilla.
  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Dunk each slice of bread in egg mixture, soaking both sides. Place in pan, and cook on both sides until golden. Serve hot. Bon Appétit!
